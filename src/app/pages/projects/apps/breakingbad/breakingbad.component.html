Breaking Bad - Android App

This project involves creating an Android application that features all the characters from the hit TV show Breaking Bad. The app will use a public API to retrieve information about each character and display it in an easy-to-use interface. The app will be designed to be used horizontally, allowing users to easily browse through the list of characters. Users will be able to search for characters by name and filter them by various criteria such as their role in the show or their affiliation with certain groups. The app will provide a fun and engaging way for fans of the show to explore the world of Breaking Bad.
