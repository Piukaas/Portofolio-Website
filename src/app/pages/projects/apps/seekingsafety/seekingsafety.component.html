Seeking Safety - KFZ

The Kwaliteit Forensische Zorg (KFZ) project aimed to develop a mobile application called "Seeking Safety" for individuals with addiction problems and/or traumas. The application included the components of the "Seeking Safety, Trauma and Addiction Handbook". The project was developed using Android Studio, a popular integrated development environment (IDE) for Android app development. The app aimed to provide users with easy access to treatment plans for their addiction and trauma-related issues. The project was focused on improving the quality of care provided to individuals struggling with addiction and trauma in the forensic care sector.
