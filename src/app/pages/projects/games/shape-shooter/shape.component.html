Shape Shooter

This 2D Unity game, developed using online tutorials, challenges players to shoot the correct shapes at incoming targets. The player must quickly identify the appropriate shape to fire in order to destroy various objects coming towards them. The game has a simple, addicting gameplay loop that's easy to pick up but hard to master. With the push of a button, players launch their ammo and rack up points while avoiding obstacles. With sharp visuals, intuitive controls, and a focus on skill-based gameplay, this 2D shooter is sure to provide a satisfying challenge for players of all levels.
