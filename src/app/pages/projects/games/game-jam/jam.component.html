Cookie Clicker

This is a captivating 2D game developed in Unity for a thrilling Game Jam challenge. Step into the world of addictive confectionery as you embark on a cookie clicker adventure infused with gambling elements. Take risks, place bets, and aim to amass a fortune in delectable treats. The game's enticing mechanics offer an easy-to-learn, yet engaging experience, keeping players hooked for hours. With polished controls and immersive gameplay, Cookie Clicker will test your luck, strategy, and sweet tooth. Can you become the ultimate cookie tycoon in this addictive gambling extravaganza?
