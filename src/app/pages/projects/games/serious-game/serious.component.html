Het Leven in Handen

Het Leven in Handen is an immersive Unity game designed to provide policymakers with a unique perspective on the impact of Artificial Intelligence. Step into the shoes of an AI responsible for making critical decisions for Cardiovascular disease patients. Through a series of scenarios, players must carefully answer yes or no, knowing that each choice has significant consequences for patient well-being. Incorrect decisions lead to engaging CPR minigames, while correct choices unlock thrilling maze challenges. With its thought-provoking gameplay and strategic decision-making, this game offers policymakers an interactive experience to better understand the complexities of AI in healthcare.
