Stel je Vraag LIVE! - CM.com

CM.com is based in Breda and develops a platform for mobile communication and payments used by customers worldwide. They offer solutions for SMS, WhatsApp, Viber, WeChat, Telegram, Facebook Messenger, Apple Business Chat, RCS, and innovative payment options. The company is looking for an online tool that allows visitors to log in and share questions during live presentations. The tool aims to solve the problem of the presenter not repeating questions asked by the audience. By using WebRTC technology, the presenter can hear the questions while the audience can also listen to the question asked.
