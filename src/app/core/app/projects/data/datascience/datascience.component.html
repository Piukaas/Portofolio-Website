Data Science Portfolio

The Data Science portfolio assignment requires the use of Python on two online datasets to discover relationships and patterns. The portfolio will contain multiple assignments, each showcasing different data analysis techniques and methods. The projects will demonstrate the ability to manipulate and analyze data, visualize data, and draw insights from the results. The portfolio will serve as evidence of proficiency in data science and the ability to apply it to real-world problems. The final product will showcase a range of skills and knowledge in the field of data science.
